How this is computed

Bus Factor for a topic = number of people whose Topic Authority Score (TAS) exceeds the topic-specific critical threshold (top quartile of activity in that topic over a rolling 60-day window). TAS is computed as a balanced composite: doc/RFC authorship 1.00 · code authorship 0.85 · closed Jira ownership 0.65 · substantive PR review 0.50 · substantive Slack thread 0.20. Recency half-life: 6 months. Critical flag is VPE-set during onboarding and quarterly review (10 topics flagged by Jordan Reeves on 2026-04-12; auto-suggestions from Jira initiative hierarchy reviewed). All outputs are reproducible with classifier version tax-2.4.0 / model-cohere-v3.
